about us

Waihura Dairy Farm is a premier dairy farm located in the beautiful countryside of Nyeri. Our farm is dedicated to providing you with the freshest and highest quality dairy products possible. We offer a range of products, from milk to cheese, all crafted with care from our healthy, happy cows. Our farm is situated near the heart of Nyeri town, making it convenient for you to enjoy farm-fresh dairy products while still being close to local shops and dining. We are committed to delivering the best dairy experience possible and look forward to welcoming you to Waihura Dairy Dairy Farm.
